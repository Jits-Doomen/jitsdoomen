Heat-Emissive Material Research (HEMR)

Abandoned Project · Phase-A Concept · 2025

Overview

A hypothetical high-emissivity thermal material for spacecraft applications. According to a Phase-A concept design, the material achieves a thermal emissivity of 0.96 while remaining cheap and thin. The project has since been abandoned and will not be updated further.

Layer Structure

  • Outer layer: Ceramic-doped silica — blocks UV and atomic oxygen
  • Main layer: Porous polymers — primary structural and thermal body
  • Inner layer: Silica aerogel — insulation and thermal decoupling
