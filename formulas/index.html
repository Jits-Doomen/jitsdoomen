D Formula Reference Sheet

Project Overview

The D Formula Reference Sheet is an open-source collection of fundamental equations and constants. It is designed to serve as a quick-reference resource for students, researchers, and engineers.

Core Disciplines

  • Physics: Classical mechanics, thermodynamics, and electromagnetism.
  • Astrophysics: Stellar properties and cosmological constants.
  • Orbital Mechanics: Keplerian motion, delta-v calculations, and maneuvers.
  • Rocket Science: Propulsion theory, staging, and nozzle dynamics.
  • Mechanical Engineering: Material properties, stress/strain, and kinematics.

Status: Active / Version 1.0.2
