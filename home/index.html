Independent Research

Dedicated to the engineering and analysis of deep-space infrastructure. My work focuses on the intersection of orbital mechanics, spacecraft architecture, and in-situ resource utilization (ISRU) to advance the technical foundations of future human expansion.

Current Focus

I am currently developing ICON (ISRU Centrifugal Orbital Node), a conceptual station design for long-duration habitation in Lunar orbit. This project serves as a testbed for integrating autonomous life support, artificial gravity systems, and ISRU-derived logistics.
